## Design Context

### Users
A self-driven learner and builder in the AI space who wants to track what top builders, researchers, and founders are saying—without algorithmic noise. They use this tool to quickly scan curated X/Twitter posts, podcast episodes, and blog posts, then drill down into topics that matter. The job to be done: stay informed efficiently, with personal filtering control.

### Brand Personality
- **Bold editorial**: strong typography, clear hierarchy, magazine-like layouts
- **Purposeful**: every UI element serves the reading and filtering flow
- **Technical but warm**: content-first, not cold or sterile

### Aesthetic Direction
- Visual tone: bold editorial with confident type, generous whitespace, and intentional contrast
- Theme: auto system-level dark/light preference, but polished in both modes
- No generic SaaS look—custom, distinctive UI that feels like a curated digest
- Reference: none (custom design tailored to the feed-browser concept)

### Design Principles
1. **Content is king**: typography and spacing should make long-form tweets and summaries effortless to read
2. **Filter without friction**: all controls visible, instantly responsive, no page reloads
3. **Confident contrast**: use strong headline weights, clear separation between sections, and intentional color accents
4. **System-native respect**: honor the user's light/dark mode preference while adding a manual toggle override
5. **Link back to source**: every card leads to the original—no content is trapped
